# Charades

Play at: **https://charades-game-blue.vercel.app**

## How to Play

1. **Choose a category** — 25 to pick from: Movies, Animals, Actions, Famous People, Countries, Famous Cities, Household Items, Computer Terminology, Universities, Sports, Food & Drink, Occupations, Legal Terms, US Presidents, European Cities, Authors, TV Shows, World Landmarks, Chinese Famous People, Chinese Cities, Chinese Universities, Chinese Emperors, Chinese Landmarks, Chinese Politicians, Climbing, or Random Mixed (a bit of everything).
2. **Choose a round length** — 30s, 60s, 90s, 2m, 3m, 4m, or 5m.
3. Hit **Start Round**. You'll see a short description of the category and its rules — tap anywhere to begin, then a 3-2-1 countdown starts the clock.
4. One player acts out (or describes, depending on the category) the word on screen while everyone else guesses. Whoever is holding the phone scores it:
   - Tap the **right side** when the group guesses correctly — the word flashes green.
   - Tap the **left side** to pass and skip to the next word — it flashes red.
5. The timer counts down continuously and turns orange with 10 seconds left. When it hits zero, the round ends automatically. You can also tap the **✕** in the corner to bail out to the main menu early.
6. See your final **Correct** vs **Passed** tally, plus every word you got right or passed listed out, then **Play Again** with the same settings or start a **New Round** to change the category or timer.

Words you've already seen won't repeat in later rounds during the same visit — refresh the page to reset that and start fresh.

No accounts, no sign-up, no rotating your phone — just pick your settings and play.
